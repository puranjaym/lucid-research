Regulatory

Navigating Structural & Ethical Risks.

DPDP Act 2023 Compliance

Stringent requirements for user consent in voice data collection. "Right to Erasure" and data residency are technical non-negotiables.

Biometric Security

The risk of AI-cloned voices for financial fraud. Requirement for robust "Proof of Liveness" in voice-activated banking.

Strategic Safeguards

  • • Privacy by Design: On-device processing to minimize cloud exposure.
  • • Bias Audits: Regular testing for dialect representation and accuracy.

Trust is a brittle asset. Compliance is the only way to scale the "Next Billion" users.
